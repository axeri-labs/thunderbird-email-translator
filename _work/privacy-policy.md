# Email Translator — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 2026-06-18*

## What data is processed

When you translate an email, the **text content of that email** is sent to a third-party
translation service for processing. No other data (headers, sender, recipient, subject,
attachments) is transmitted.

## Which services receive your data

Depending on the translation provider selected in the extension settings:

- **MyMemory** (default) — data is sent to `api.mymemory.translated.net`, operated by
  Translated S.r.l. (Italy). See their privacy policy at https://mymemory.translated.net/
- **DeepL** (optional) — data is sent to `api-free.deepl.com`, operated by DeepL SE
  (Germany). See their privacy policy at https://www.deepl.com/privacy

## Local storage

Translated results are cached locally in your Thunderbird profile using the browser's
`storage.local` API. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the
extension developer.

## What the developer does NOT collect

- No usage data, telemetry, or analytics are collected.
- No email content or translation results are stored on any server operated by the developer.
- No account or registration is required to use this extension.
