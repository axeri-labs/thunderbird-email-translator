Privacy Policy

Email Translator for Thunderbird — Last updated: 2026-06-20

What data is processed

When you translate an email, the text content of that email is sent to the translation service you selected in the extension settings. No other data (headers, sender, recipient, subject, attachments) is transmitted.

Which services receive your data

Depending on the translation provider selected in Settings:

  • Google Translate (default) — data is sent to translate.googleapis.com, operated by Google LLC. See Google's privacy policy.
  • MyMemory (optional) — data is sent to api.mymemory.translated.net, operated by Translated S.r.l. (Italy). See their privacy policy.
  • DeepL (optional) — data is sent to api-free.deepl.com, operated by DeepL SE (Germany). See their privacy policy.

Local storage

Translated results are cached locally in your Thunderbird profile using the browser's storage.local API. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the extension developer.

What the developer does not collect

  • No usage data, telemetry, or analytics are collected.
  • No email content or translation results are stored on any server operated by the developer.
  • No account or registration is required to use this extension.
